Need about us statement/mission statement

Maureen Groll

I have had a passion for health and fitness as long as I can remember! I am lucky enough to have been able to turn my favorite hobby into an interesting and rewarding career that means the world to me!

As a certified group fitness instructor and personal trainer my experience includes teaching fitness classes at several area gyms managing the Fitness Center at a local facility, conducting personal training sessions with clients ranging in age from pre-teen to 70+ years of age and acting as the Strength and Conditioning Coach for a nearby high school.

I am a 53 year old mother of four adult children. I understand firsthand the challenges of blending your fitness and health goals with the demands of a job, a constantly changing schedule and a busy family! Schedule an appointment at Living Fitness and I will use both my personal and professional experience to help develop a fitness for you that will work and that will help you feel happier, healthier, more confident and more energetic! I promise you… fitness can be FUN!! It’s never too late to start!
